Avoidable food waste in Norway

This bar chart shows avoidable food waste for the baseline scenario (S0) using amount of food waste from 2010 in Norway (Matsvinn i Norge (2011)). The avoidable food waste is split into amount of tons of avoidable food waste for the different processes filtered by content for each process. The total amount of avoidable food waste for the Norwegian system is 384 693 tons

Source: Matsvinn i Norge (2011)
