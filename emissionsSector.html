Emissions from Norwegian territory

Source: http://ssb.no/klimagassn/

Emissions from Norwegian territory: Norwegian agriculture is estimated to contribute to around 4.7 ton co2 eqv (SSB 2015). This is emissions directly linked to the agriculture sector and amounts to around 9% of the total emissions coming from Norwegian territory. Further on, NILF together with MiSA has estimated that total emissions linked to agriculture to be close up to 15% Universitetet for miljø og biovitenskap (Trømborg, Nielsen og Hoen (red) 2008) if additional estimates are measured. Further on of the total emissions, using lifecycle analysis as a methodology, could also increase this amount, as the indirect GHG emissions from producing food, all the way from the soli to the table (NILF and MiSA report).

Source: http://ssb.no/klimagassn/ + NILF + MiSA
