Comparison of phosphorus, energy and dry matter scenarios



This is a comparison of phosphorus, energy and dry matter for the different scenarios (Hamilton, 2015). With comparing effects of different scenarios for the selected flows, we will try to higlight the importance of being aware of the fact that problemshifting may occur. If you want to select a certain process, press and hold the line, and it will get a dark color. That way you can compare this process more clearly with other processes.
