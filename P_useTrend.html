Phosphorus and Nitrogen use in Norway from 2008 - 2014

This chart shows the use of mineralfertilizer in Norway over time.

Mineralfertilizer use in Norway over time (Showing total turnover)

This data is based on the total turnover for mineralfertilizer used in agriculture. The data is based on rapporting from the retailers selling mineral fertilier. With this we see a general upward trend in phosphorus and nitrogen use. Incresing amounts of food waste might also contribute to increasing this even more.

Source: Mattilsynet (2008-2014)
