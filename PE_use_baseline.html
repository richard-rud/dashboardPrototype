Food production is driving energy use

This figure shows the process energy use for the agriculture sector and biogas production using the average from the years 2009 - 2011.

Energy use in agriculture production


Increased food production leads to increased use of energy. By reducing overall food waste we also reduce food production, reducing the energy demand. We can from this figure especially see that producing meat ("PE for feed production" and "PE for meat processing") and dairy products ("PE dairy products manufacturing") leads to a high demand for energy

Source: Hamilton et al. 2015
